Our History

The Story of Techtrix

Techtrix Solutions, founded by Prashant Sathe on March 25, 2009, with the aim of providing professional services and fostering strong customer relationships. In the early days, our focus was primarily on the telecommunications sector. However, as we grew and evolved, we realized the importance of diversification. .
It was in 2011 that Subhash Kandhare joined our team, and his vision paved the way for our expansion into various sectors, including IT infrastructure, digital security solutions, software solutions, and cloud services. Despite being bootstrapped, Techtrix Solutions has thrived by leveraging a robust network, effective communication with customers, and delivering high-quality services at competitive prices.
